About Page

For Guests

  1. Create an Account
    Sign up and log in as a Guest.
  2. Find a Queue
    Enter a Queue ID, browse Popular Queues, or view Active Queues.
  3. View Queue Details
    Check status, number of people waiting, and estimated wait time.
  4. Join the Queue
    Click “Join Queue” to secure your spot remotely.
  5. Track Your Position
    Monitor your place in line and exit anytime if needed.

For Hosts

  1. Create an Account
    Sign up and log in as a Host.
  2. Create a Queue
    Provide queue name, address, estimated service time, and population limit.
  3. Share the Queue ID
    Provide the unique Queue ID to customers so they can join digitally.
  4. Manage Your Queue
    Monitor participants and control queue status.
  5. Serve Efficiently
    Call the next customer and manage the queue in real time.
